Morgan Stanley Data Analyst Intern

Rithvik Senthil CSE CEG
Read More

The online assessment had 3 sections. 1) 10 aptitude questions to be solved in 30 minutes 2) 7 debugging questions to be solved in 20 minutes 3) 3 coding questions to be solved in 1 hour The aptitude questions were of medium difficulty and the challenging part was to solve them within the given time. Questions were based on profit/loss, family member relations and numerical reasoning mainly. Few questions were longer than others and the trick here was to not spend too much time on a single question and move on to questions that consume less time.
